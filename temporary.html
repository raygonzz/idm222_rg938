At Bergo Fencing Inc., we’ve got you covered with temporary fencing for just about anything—whether it’s for security, construction safety, special events, or even privacy. When it comes to the construction industry, temporary fencing is a game-changer! It’s been loved, moving expensive materials and heavy equipment every single day isn’t exactly practical.

Plus, fencing rental use is a smart way to reduce your liability risks. Without a fence, there’s always a chance someone could wander into your job site, putting them at risk of getting caught with a whole list of unnecessary headaches.

But that’s not all—temporary fencing also keeps your site more secure, helping to prevent theft and even deter graffiti. Now we’re not saying it’s a 100% guarantee against intrusions, vandalism, or accidents, but for just a few bucks a day, why not give your project an extra layer of protection? It’s a small price to pay for peace of mind and keeping your site as safe and secure as possible.
